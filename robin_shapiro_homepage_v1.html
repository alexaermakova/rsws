About Robin

Science-backed.
Human-first.

Robin's approach to training is rooted in movement science and shaped by real clinical experience. After earning a Kinesiology degree and spending a year working alongside physical therapists at G Sports, he brings a level of anatomical understanding that most trainers simply don't have.

His style is direct, encouraging, and completely judgment-free. Whether you're recovering from an injury, building strength for the first time, or pushing for your next athletic goal — Robin designs programs that actually fit your life.
